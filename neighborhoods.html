Find Your Perfect San Diego Community

San Diego isn't just one city--it's a collection of distinct neighborhoods, each with its own character, lifestyle, and real estate market.

Born and raised here, I've spent my life exploring every corner of San Diego County. From the upscale coastal enclaves of La Jolla and Del Mar to the hip urban neighborhoods of North Park and Hillcrest, I can help you find the community that matches your lifestyle, budget, and dreams.

Coastal Communities

Beach Living at Its Finest

La Jolla

Luxury Ocean Views Top Schools

Known as "The Jewel," La Jolla offers stunning coastline, world-class dining, and some of San Diego's most prestigious addresses. Home to UCSD and the famous Cove.

$2.8M Median Price
9/10 Schools

Del Mar

Beach Town Horseracing Village Feel

A charming coastal village known for its beautiful beaches, the Del Mar Racetrack, and a relaxed yet upscale atmosphere. Perfect for those seeking beach town living.

$2.4M Median Price
10/10 Schools

Encinitas

Surf Culture Yoga Capital Family Friendly

A laid-back surf town with excellent beaches, a thriving downtown, and strong community spirit. Known as the "Yoga Capital" with a health-conscious lifestyle.

$1.8M Median Price
9/10 Schools

Solana Beach

Design District Cedros Ave Walkable

Small but stylish coastal community featuring the Cedros Design District. Great restaurants, boutique shopping, and easy beach access in a compact, walkable setting.

$2.1M Median Price
10/10 Schools

Carlsbad

LEGOLAND Flower Fields Golf

A family-friendly coastal city with excellent schools, beautiful beaches, world-famous flower fields, and attractions like LEGOLAND. Great for families and professionals.

$1.6M Median Price
9/10 Schools

Pacific Beach

Nightlife Young Professionals Boardwalk

A vibrant, youthful beach community with a famous boardwalk, lively nightlife, and endless outdoor activities. Popular with young professionals and active lifestyles.

$1.4M Median Price
7/10 Schools
North County

Space, Value & Community

Rancho Santa Fe

Equestrian Estates Privacy

One of America's wealthiest zip codes featuring sprawling estates, equestrian properties, and ultimate privacy. A haven for celebrities and executives.

$4.2M Median Price
10/10 Schools

Rancho Bernardo

Master Planned Golf Tech Hub

A well-established master-planned community with excellent amenities, top-rated schools, and easy access to major tech employers. Great for families and retirees alike.

$950K Median Price
9/10 Schools

Poway

City in the Country Trails Award Schools

Known as "The City in the Country," Poway offers larger lots, excellent schools, and a strong sense of community. Great for families who want space without sacrificing convenience.

$1.2M Median Price
10/10 Schools

San Marcos

Growing Restaurant Row CSUSM

A rapidly growing city with a revitalized downtown, home to Cal State San Marcos. Excellent value with new developments and improving amenities.

$825K Median Price
7/10 Schools

Oceanside

Beach Access Military Revitalizing

San Diego's third-largest city with miles of beaches, a historic pier, and a revitalizing downtown. Great value for beach living near Camp Pendleton.

$875K Median Price
7/10 Schools

Vista

Craft Breweries Affordable Growing

An up-and-coming community with a thriving craft brewery scene, historic downtown, and some of North County's most affordable homes.

$775K Median Price
6/10 Schools
Central San Diego

Urban Living & Character

North Park

Hip Craft Beer Walkable

One of San Diego's trendiest neighborhoods with fantastic restaurants, craft breweries, and a thriving arts scene. Walkable streets and historic Craftsman homes.

$950K Median Price
7/10 Schools

Hillcrest

LGBTQ+ Friendly Dining Central Location

San Diego's most vibrant urban neighborhood, known for its inclusive community, excellent restaurants, and central location near Balboa Park.

$875K Median Price
7/10 Schools

Normal Heights

Up & Coming Adams Ave Character

A charming neighborhood with tree-lined streets, eclectic shops along Adams Avenue, and a strong community feel. Great value with tons of character.

$825K Median Price
7/10 Schools

Clairemont

Central Family Homes Value

A centrally-located neighborhood offering mid-century homes, good schools, and excellent value. Easy access to beaches, downtown, and major freeways.

$925K Median Price
7/10 Schools

Mira Mesa

Tech Corridor Diverse Affordable

A diverse, family-friendly community along the tech corridor with good schools, diverse dining options, and some of the best values in central San Diego.

$850K Median Price
8/10 Schools

Carmel Valley

Master Planned Top Schools Shopping

A premier master-planned community featuring top-rated schools, upscale shopping at Del Mar Highlands, and beautiful newer homes. Ideal for families.

$1.6M Median Price
10/10 Schools
