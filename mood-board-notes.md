# Rau Home Group Website - Mood Board & Inspiration

## Inspiration Sites
1. **Jade Mills Estates** (jademillsestates.com)
   - Full-bleed hero with luxury property aerial shot
   - Elegant script logo
   - Minimal nav (Properties, Home Search, Menu)
   - Single strong CTA "SEARCH ALL HOMES"
   - Dark overlay for text readability

2. **The Altman Brothers** (thealtmanbrothers.com)
   - Dramatic night shot of luxury home
   - Bold stats front and center "$8.3 BILLION SOLD"
   - Gold/white on dark background
   - Douglas Elliman branding integration
   - Single CTA "VIEW OUR SOLD HOMES"

3. **The Oppenheim Group** (ogroup.com)
   - Ultra minimal - almost no text
   - Stunning interior photography
   - Location list (LA, Newport Beach, San Diego, Cabo)
   - Social icons vertical on left
   - Clean, aspirational, lets imagery speak

## Common Elements to Replicate
- Full-screen hero images (properties or skyline)
- Minimal, clean navigation
- Bold typography for headlines
- Strong personal branding
- Single prominent CTA
- Dark overlays for text on images
- Stats/credentials prominently displayed
- Luxury, aspirational aesthetic
