About Imperial Beach

Imperial Beach is California's most southwesterly city, offering an authentic, laid-back beach town atmosphere. The community features great surf, the longest wooden pier in California, and some of San Diego's most affordable beach living.

The Imperial Beach Pier is a local landmark and fishing spot. Tijuana Sloughs National Wildlife Refuge offers nature trails. The beach provides consistent surf and hosts the annual U.S. Open Sandcastle Competition. The community maintains a local, non-touristy vibe.

Real estate includes beach-close homes, apartments, and condos at prices significantly lower than northern beach communities. The surf culture, affordability, and authentic beach town feel attract surfers, artists, and those seeking unpretentious coastal living.
