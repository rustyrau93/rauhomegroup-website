About Chula Vista

Chula Vista is San Diego County's second-largest city, offering diverse neighborhoods from bayfront developments to established suburban areas. The city provides good value, improving amenities, and proximity to the border.

The bayfront features the Chula Vista Marina, Living Coast Discovery Center, and new residential developments with bay views. Eastlake and Otay Ranch offer master-planned communities with parks and trails. Third Avenue downtown is revitalizing.

Real estate ranges from affordable condos and starter homes to newer master-planned communities and bayfront properties. The variety, value, and growth make Chula Vista attractive to diverse buyers.
