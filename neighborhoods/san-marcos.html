About San Marcos

San Marcos is a rapidly growing city in North County featuring Cal State San Marcos, an improving downtown, and excellent value. The community is investing in infrastructure and attracting new residents and businesses.

Downtown San Marcos is revitalizing with new restaurants, shops, and the popular 'Restaurant Row' along San Marcos Boulevard. Discovery Lake provides recreation, and the city is adding parks and trails. CSUSM brings educational and cultural opportunities.

Real estate includes newer developments, established neighborhoods, and townhomes at various price points. The growth, affordability, and improving amenities make San Marcos attractive to first-time buyers and families.
