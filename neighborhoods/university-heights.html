About University Heights

University Heights sits on a mesa overlooking Mission Valley, offering hilltop views, excellent walkability, and a growing craft beer scene. The neighborhood combines historic charm with modern amenities.

Park Boulevard features breweries, restaurants, coffee shops, and boutiques. Trolley Barn Park is a community gathering spot. The neighborhood's elevated position provides views and cooler temperatures than the valleys below.

Real estate includes Craftsman bungalows, Spanish-style homes, and some modern builds. The walkability, dining options, and central location make University Heights popular with young professionals and couples.
