About Oceanside

Oceanside is San Diego County's third-largest city, offering 3.5 miles of sandy beaches, a historic wooden pier, and some of the most affordable coastal real estate in the region. The city is experiencing a major revitalization.

Downtown Oceanside has transformed in recent years with craft breweries, art galleries, and new restaurants. The historic pier and harbor area offer fishing, surfing, and waterfront dining. The city's proximity to Camp Pendleton provides a strong military presence and community.

Real estate includes beachfront properties, downtown condos, and suburban neighborhoods. The revitalizing downtown and improving amenities make Oceanside an excellent value for those seeking beach access without premium coastal prices.

Who Lives Here

Oceanside attracts military families, surfers, young professionals seeking coastal value, and those working at Camp Pendleton. The community is diverse, active, and increasingly focused on arts and culture.
