About Solana Beach

Solana Beach is a small but stylish coastal gem known for the Cedros Design District, beautiful beaches, and a tight-knit community feel. This walkable beach town offers sophistication in a compact package.

Stretching just 3.5 square miles along the coast, Solana Beach punches above its weight in terms of amenities and lifestyle. The Cedros Avenue Design District features high-end furniture stores, art galleries, and unique boutiques, while the downtown area offers excellent restaurants and craft breweries.

Real estate ranges from bluff-top homes with ocean views to modern condos near the train station, making it popular with commuters to downtown San Diego. The community's walkability, top-rated schools, and proximity to both beach and urban amenities make it ideal for families and professionals.

Who Lives Here

Solana Beach attracts design-conscious professionals, young families, and active retirees who appreciate walkability and coastal access. The community values art, design, sustainability, and maintaining its small-town character.
