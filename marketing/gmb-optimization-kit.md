## Pre-Seeded Q&A

### Questions to Post:

**Q1:** What areas of San Diego do you serve?
**A:** I serve all of San Diego County, with special expertise in La Jolla, Del Mar, Carmel Valley, Scripps Ranch, Encinitas, North Park, and the coastal communities. Whether you're buying or selling, I can help throughout the San Diego area.

**Q2:** Do you help first-time home buyers?
**A:** Absolutely! I specialize in helping first-time buyers navigate the San Diego market. I'll guide you through the entire process, from getting pre-approved to closing, and help you find down payment assistance programs you may qualify for.

**Q3:** What is the average home price in San Diego?
**A:** As of 2025, the median home price in San Diego is approximately $950,000. However, prices vary significantly by neighborhood - from $600K in some inland areas to $3M+ in coastal communities like La Jolla and Del Mar. I can help you find areas that match your budget.

**Q4:** How long does it take to buy a house in San Diego?
**A:** The typical timeline is 3-6 months from starting your search to closing. This includes getting pre-approved (1-2 weeks), finding the right home (1-4 months), and the escrow/closing process (30-45 days).

**Q5:** Do you offer free home valuations?
**A:** Yes! I provide complimentary home value analyses for San Diego homeowners. I'll look at recent comparable sales, market trends, and your home's unique features to give you an accurate estimate. Contact me to schedule yours.

**Q6:** What makes you different from other San Diego agents?
**A:** I'm a San Diego native with deep local knowledge, combined with data-driven market analysis. I focus on educating my clients so they can make confident decisions. I'm with the Rau Home Group, one of San Diego's top teams.

**Q7:** Are you available on weekends?
**A:** Yes! Real estate doesn't stop on weekends. I'm available 7 days a week for showings, consultations, and client needs. Weekend appointments are often the most convenient for working buyers.

**Q8:** Do you help with relocation to San Diego?
**A:** Yes, relocation is one of my specialties. Moving to San Diego from out of state? I help with neighborhood selection, virtual tours, and coordinating everything so your move is smooth. Many of my clients have never visited before closing!

**Q9:** What should I look for in a San Diego neighborhood?
**A:** It depends on your priorities! Key factors include: school quality, commute times, beach access, home prices, and lifestyle (urban vs suburban). I help clients identify what matters most and match them with the right neighborhoods.

**Q10:** How do I get started working with you?
**A:** Easy! Just send me a message through Google, call my number, or visit my website. I offer free consultations to discuss your goals and create a personalized plan, whether you're buying, selling, or just exploring options.
