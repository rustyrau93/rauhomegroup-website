The Process

Your Path to Homeownership

A clear roadmap from search to keys

01

Initial Consultation

  • Discuss your goals, needs, and timeline
  • Understand your must-haves and nice-to-haves
  • Review the buying process together
  • Identify neighborhoods that match your lifestyle
  • Answer all your questions
02

Financial Preparation

  • Connect you with trusted lenders
  • Get pre-approved for a mortgage
  • Understand your buying power
  • Review loan options (conventional, VA, FHA)
  • Set a realistic budget
03

The Home Search

  • Set up custom property alerts
  • Access to listings before they hit the market
  • Tour homes at your pace
  • Provide honest feedback and insights
  • Find the right home—not just any home
04

Making Your Offer

  • Analyze comparable sales
  • Craft a competitive offer strategy
  • Write a strong, compelling offer
  • Negotiate price and terms
  • Get your offer accepted
05

Due Diligence

  • Coordinate home inspection
  • Review inspection reports together
  • Negotiate repairs or credits if needed
  • Review all disclosures and title
  • Finalize your mortgage
06

Closing & Keys

  • Final walk-through of the property
  • Sign closing documents
  • Transfer utilities and services
  • Get your keys and celebrate!
  • Welcome home
